Why FEI TeamArt

A learning system built through making, reference, and reflection.

Reference-First Learning

Students begin from artworks, observations, and structured seeing before building their own work.

Public-Domain & Open-Access Roots

Many LFC pathways begin with pre-1945 works, PD/CC0 references, and museum collections.

Make, Reflect, Grow

Learning is not passive. Students compare, make, reflect, and build personal direction over time.

Badges as Growth Markers

Badges connect creative milestones across courses, pathways, and the student portal journey.
